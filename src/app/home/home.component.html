Octopay is a platform that allows participants of Epoch 1.0 purchase and use the event pass as well as octocoins.
Event pass gives access to the various assortment of events planned out during Epoch.
OctoCoins are the virtual coins which can be used at multiple food stalls, games and events. 10 OctoCoins = 20 INR.
Add OctoCoins to your account using online payment methods. We also have a surprise for the person who spent the most OctoCoins
